Tanjung Bira adalah pantai yang terletak di Kecamatan Bonto Bahari, sekitar 40 km dari Kota Bulukumba. Pantai yang terkenal dengan pasir putihnya ini sangat nyaman dan mudah dijangkau. Tanjung Bira sangat menarik, para pegunjung dapat melihat para nelayan dengan perahu mereka melaut di malam hari. Ada banyak fasilitas tersedia di Tanjung Bira seperti hotel, villa, dan restorang sepanjang pantai. Tanjung Bira is a beach located in Kecamatan Bonto Bahari, about 40 km from Kota Bulukumba. The beach known for its white sand that is very&nbsp; convenient and easy to reach. Tanjung Bira is&nbsp; very attractive that you can see fishermen with small boats come and go to fish at night. There are many facilities available in Tanjung Bira such as hotels, villas, and restaurants along the coast.
